Benefits of Yoga

Improves Flexibility

Regular practice increases joint mobility and reduces stiffness

Reduces Stress

Yoga lowers cortisol levels and promotes relaxation

Enhances Breathing

Pranayama techniques improve lung capacity and oxygenation

Strengthens Core

Builds abdominal strength and improves posture

Recommended Yoga Routines

Stress Relief Sequence

  1. Child's Pose (Balasana) - 1 minute
  2. Cat-Cow Stretch (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana) - 2 minutes
  3. Standing Forward Bend (Uttanasana) - 1 minute
  4. Legs-Up-the-Wall Pose (Viparita Karani) - 5 minutes
  5. Corpse Pose (Savasana) - 5-10 minutes

Energy Boost Sequence

  1. Sun Salutations (Surya Namaskar) - 5 rounds
  2. Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I) - 30 seconds each side
  3. Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II) - 30 seconds each side
  4. Tree Pose (Vrksasana) - 1 minute each side
  5. Bridge Pose (Setu Bandha Sarvangasana) - 1 minute

Better Sleep Sequence

  1. Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana) - 2 minutes
  2. Reclining Butterfly (Supta Baddha Konasana) - 3 minutes
  3. Supine Spinal Twist (Supta Matsyendrasana) - 1 minute each side
  4. Legs-Up-the-Wall Pose (Viparita Karani) - 5 minutes
  5. Corpse Pose (Savasana) - 5-10 minutes

Flexibility Sequence

  1. Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana) - 1 minute
  2. Low Lunge (Anjaneyasana) - 1 minute each side
  3. Pyramid Pose (Parsvottanasana) - 1 minute each side
  4. Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana) - 2 minutes
  5. Bound Angle Pose (Baddha Konasana) - 2 minutes
